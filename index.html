About this page

This page was created in support of the outputs from the Research Data Alliance Research Metadata Schemas Working Group. Specifically, a crosswalk of schema.org properties and corresponding metadata terms. Monash student Penelope Hagan collated all crosswalks collected from a survey, instrumented by the RDA Research Metadata Schemas WG. The tables below represent the crosswalk when it was ingested into the site in early September 2020 and is not a live read of the original table. The page is intended as a prototype service for the research data management community in support of managers who are investigating options for including schema.org markup into existing well formed metadata.

This table is a free text search over both metadata terms and schema.org properties. Wildcard searches are not supported but partial searches are.
